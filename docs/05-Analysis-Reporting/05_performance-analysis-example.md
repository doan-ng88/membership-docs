## Overview

The Performance Analysis feature provides comprehensive analytics on your email and communication campaigns. It helps you measure effectiveness, track engagement, and optimize future campaigns based on data.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- Manager or Admin access rights
- Completed email campaigns to analyze
- Understanding of key email metrics
- Goals for your email marketing efforts

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Access Performance Analysis

1. Log in to the administration system
2. From the left menu, select **Communication Management > Mail Integration > Performance Analysis**

<!-- ![Access Performance Analysis](./img/performance-analysis-access.png) -->

### 2. View Campaign Performance

1. In the **"Campaign Overview"** section, select:
   - Date range for analysis
   - Specific campaigns or all campaigns
   - Metrics to display

2. View key performance metrics:
   - **Delivery Rate**: Percentage of emails successfully delivered
   - **Open Rate**: Percentage of recipients who opened the email
   - **Click Rate**: Percentage who clicked links
   - **Conversion Rate**: Percentage who completed desired actions
   - **Unsubscribe Rate**: Percentage who opted out
   - **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of undeliverable emails

3. Compare metrics against:
   - Previous time periods
   - Industry benchmarks
   - Your own past performance

<!-- ![View Campaign Performance](./img/campaign-performance.png) -->

### 3. Analyze Member Engagement

1. In the **"Member Engagement"** section:
   - View engagement by member segment
   - Track engagement over time
   - Identify most engaged members

2. Analyze engagement patterns:
   - Time of day with highest open rates
   - Days of week with best performance
   - Devices used to read emails
   - Email clients most frequently used

3. View engagement heat maps and charts

<!-- ![Analyze Member Engagement](./img/member-engagement.png) -->

### 4. Track Conversion Metrics

1. In the **"Conversion Tracking"** section:
   - View conversion funnel visualizations
   - Track specific conversion events
   - Measure ROI of email campaigns

2. Analyze what drives conversions:
   - Subject line effectiveness
   - Call-to-action performance
   - Email content and layout impact
   - Timing effects

3. View attribution data for multi-touch conversions

<!-- ![Track Conversion Metrics](./img/conversion-metrics.png) -->

### 5. Export Reports

1. Customize reports by selecting:
   - Metrics to include
   - Date range
   - Comparison periods
   - Chart types
   - Segmentation

2. Choose export format:
   - PDF report
   - Excel spreadsheet
   - CSV data file
   - Google Data Studio connection

3. Schedule automated reports:
   - Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
   - Specify recipients
   - Customize email message

<!-- ![Export Reports](./img/export-performance-reports.png) -->

## Best Practices

- **Compare performance against previous campaigns** to track improvement
- **Segment analysis by member levels** to understand different engagement patterns
- **Look for correlations between email design and performance**
- **Test different send times** to optimize engagement
- **Set up regular automated reports** for stakeholders
- **Use A/B test results** to inform future campaign decisions

## Troubleshooting

- **Missing data in reports**:
  - Check tracking code implementation
  - Verify date range selection
  - Ensure campaigns have sufficient data

- **Inconsistent metrics**:
  - Check for definition changes between reports
  - Verify tracking consistency across campaigns
  - Ensure proper attribution settings

## FAQ

**Q**: How long does it take for campaign data to appear in reports?
**A**: Basic metrics (opens, clicks) appear within a few hours, while conversion data may take 24-48 hours to fully process.

**Q**: Can I create custom reports for specific stakeholders?
**A**: Yes, you can create and save report templates with specific metrics and visualizations for different audiences.

**Q**: How do you calculate ROI for email campaigns?
**A**: The system tracks conversions and their associated revenue, then calculates ROI based on the campaign cost you input.
